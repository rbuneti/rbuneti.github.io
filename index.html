Overview

Texture is an AI-powered shopping discovery app designed for college students and Gen Z shoppers. Instead of overwhelming users with endless product feeds, Texture uses conversational AI to understand a user's mood and context, then curates emotionally aligned product recommendations.

Problem Statement

College students and Gen Z shoppers often use online shopping as an emotional and exploratory activity, but current platforms feel impersonal, cluttered, and overwhelming. Users are forced to scroll endlessly, compare unreliable reviews, and manage decision fatigue without meaningful guidance.

Why This Matters

Poorly aligned recommendations lead to frustration, impulsive purchases, and regret. A mood-aware approach can help users feel confident, supported, and intentional while preserving the joy of discovery.

Target Users

  • College students and Gen Z shoppers
  • Online shopping for clothing, accessories, and lifestyle products
